Now Arriving: The new PDX Transit.

Re-written from the ground up, this new generation of PDX Transit was built on Apple's latest technologies and frameworks delivering speed, stability, and a new foundation that will carry it for the next 10 years.

Version 1.1 featuring Live Activities now available!


Arrival Times

Get real-time arrivals for any TriMet or Portland Streetcar stop through an immersive, detail-rich interface. Explore select stops with 360° panoramic views, stay informed with live service alerts from TriMet, and bookmark your favorites for instant access. You can even set 3- or 5-minute push notification reminders so you never miss your ride.
