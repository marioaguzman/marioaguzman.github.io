for Macintosh

Stay connected to Portland’s public transportation — right from your desktop. PDX Transit is a native Mac app that gives you real-time arrival times, service alerts, and nearby transit stops in one seamless experience. Designed exclusively for macOS, it delivers the speed and polish you expect from the Macintosh platform.

Now Available: PDX Transit for macOS Tahoe

Nearby Transit Stops & Arrival Times

Get nearby TriMet transit stop details and arrival times in real-time.

Bookmarks

Bookmark your favorite or most-frequented transit stops for convenient access.

Service Alerts

View all service alerts published by TriMet conveniently grouped by transit line.

Dark Mode

Because PDX Transit is a fully native app, it beautifully adapts to system settings and modes, including Dark Mode.

macOS Compatibility

PDX Transit for Mac is compatible with macOS 13 "Ventura" and newer.

Download for free today

Lightweight. No accounts. No sign-ups. No AI. No telemetry. Built entirely with native Apple frameworks.
